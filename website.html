About Black People Transit


BPT

Black People Transit also known as BPT helps many black families getting transportation.Our solution is getting sponsors and donations from other buissnesses or people.We provide bikes and public buses from the money recieve.We are a non profit organization.We rely on donations and sponsors from people.Our donations range from $1 to $50.Even 1 dollar can make a difference for black families.
